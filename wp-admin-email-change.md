WP Admin Email update guide

    Step01. Enter into your server
    Step02. Go to cPanel whatever you have
    Step03. Find ot database
    Step04. Open phpMyAdmin
    Step05. Find out wp_options and click on it
    Step06. Ctrl+F and type: admin_email (You will probably see 2 entries with admin_email and admin_email_lifespan)
    Step07. Only updtae admin_email
    Step08. After changing it there is a button "Go". Click on this and you have done this. ☺️👌
